The Scout is the best all-rounder class in the game. Normally, he's an annoying
Bostonian that does everything fast: he runs fast, he hits fast, he dies fast.
In TFTag, he's still fast, but can still be caught by enemies using help from
their team, or better movement.

His items are similar, but slightly different from the vanilla game.
